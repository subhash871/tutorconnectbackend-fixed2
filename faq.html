For Tutors

To maintain high standards, we require a copy of your Citizenship (Nagarpalika/CDO issued), your highest academic degree (Transcript/Provisional), and a professional headshot. If you are teaching SEE/NEB levels, experience in those curriculums is highly preferred.

We charge a flat 15% service fee on every session. This fee covers platform maintenance, marketing your profile to students across Nepal, and secure payment processing via eSewa and Khalti.

Payments & Billing

We support all major Nepalese payment gateways including:

  • eSewa Wallet
  • Khalti Wallet
  • ConnectIPS
  • Fonepay (Direct QR)
  • Bank Transfers (NIBL, NIC Asia, Global IME)

Tutors can request a payout once their balance reaches NPR 2,000. Payouts are processed every Friday directly to your linked eSewa account or Bank Account. It typically takes 1-2 business days for the funds to reflect in your bank.
