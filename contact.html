Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform.

View all FAQs arrow_forward

How do I book a session?

Browse our category of tutors, select a profile you like, and use their integrated calendar to book an available slot instantly.

What are the payment methods?

We support major local wallets like eSewa and Khalti, along with direct bank transfers and international card payments.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before the session starts for a full refund back to your platform wallet.
